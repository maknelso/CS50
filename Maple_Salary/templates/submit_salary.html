Please enter all information in, if not applicable for your job position, just type in "0". For Restricted Share Units (RSU), please use average fair market value (FMV) divided by vesting schedule years to get average FMV per year.

E.g., you get 8 RSUs at FMV of $100. Vesting is over 4 years. Therefore, on average, you get 2 RSUs per year (8 RSU / 4 years). Therefore, on average, the FMV of your RSU is valued at 2 RSUs per year * $100 FMV at the time of your offer or $200. You therefore, enter 200 in RSU box.
